Instructions:
  • Start: After pressing "Start", click the top left corner and bottom right corner of the "page" on your preferred text editor (not the corner of the browser). This will take a screenshot of your text editor and will periodically check for inactivity. So get to work!!
  • Stop: Hopefully, you aren't procrastinating and perhaps doing research, in that case, press the "Stop" button and it will pause the periodic checks so you aren't flagged for inactivity. (Be honest, don't cheat yourself!)
  • Resize: You may have clicked the same spot twice or didn't read what pressing "Start" did and messed up your bonds, it happens. No worries, you can redo your bounds by using the "Resize" button. (Remember, top left corner and bottom right corner of your page)
